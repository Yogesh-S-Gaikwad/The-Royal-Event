Sangeet & Mehndi Functions

Sangeet and Mehndi functions are blended to save time & resources while minimizing the additional costs of individual functions. The women normally attend the bridal Mehendi, all the attendance takes part in selecting their Mehendi designs, which involves choosing from a range of Indian and Arabic styles. Interestingly, the use of Mehndi is linked to a number of themes. For example, it is said that the darker the bride's Mehndi, the more her husband would love her. It is also customary to keep the bride and groom's names secret during the ceremony. The Sangeet is one of the most lively and enjoyable concepts of an Indian wedding. For most cultures, Hindu traditions regard music as the lifeblood of a wedding ceremony, and the Sangeet event is no exception, with joyful dancing and singing set to live music. Professional singers who can belt out famous movie songs as well as traditional wedding songs are frequently invited to perform at this crucial Indian pre-wedding event.
